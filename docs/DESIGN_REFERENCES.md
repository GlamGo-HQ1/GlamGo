## 📱 Section: Landing Page / App Anticipation

**Concept Name:** "Cinematic Typography Mask (The Obsidian & Tiwis Effect)"

**1. Reference URL:** `https://obsidianassembly.com/` & `https://www.tiwis.fr/en`
**2. The Vibe:**

- **The Inspiration:** A masterclass in scroll-linked micro-interactions and "heavy" smooth scrolling. Unlike standard websites, these sites feel incredibly robust and physical to navigate.
- **The Interaction (Zoom Mask):** The Obsidian site uses massive, screen-filling typography that masks a video layer beneath it. As the user scrubs downward, words (or letters) expand/zoom continuously until the open space of a letter completely swallows the screen, revealing the full video.
- **The Application:** We use this as a potential high-impact Hero variant (Variant B in our Strategy doc) to make the GlamGo title expand into our own high-definition haircut/transformation video.

**1. Reference Image:** `fabletics_video_reel.png` (captured from chat)

**1. The Vibe:**
- Instead of static thumbnails, every card is alive. 
- A hero video (3-5 seconds) plays on hover to show the hair "in motion" (360-degree turn or natural movement).
- **Detail View:** When clicked, shows 4-5 different angles, with at least one more detailed video clip.

---

**Concept Name:** "Inset Angle Gallery Preview (The 'See Both' Card)"

**1. Reference Image:** `overlapping_inset_card.png` (captured from chat)
**2. The Vibe:**
- **The Layout:** In the main gallery grid (where users are browsing many styles/stylists), the cards aren't just single squares. They feature a large **primary photo** (usually the front/face) and a smaller, slightly overlapping **inset photo** tucked in the bottom right corner (showing the back or detail).
- **The Purpose:** It immediately fulfills the core promise of GlamGo: *seeing all the angles*. The user instantly sees the front and back of the style without even having to click. 
- **The Wow Factor:** This breaks up the boring, uniform grid look of standard directories (like Instagram or Pinterest) and makes the gallery look like a high-end editorial lookbook.

## 📸 Section: Gallery Category View

**Concept Name:** "Category Hero Banner"

**1. Reference Image:** `fabletics_category_banner.png` (captured from chat)
**2. The Vibe:**
- **The Trigger:** When a user taps on a specific category tag (e.g., "Braids") to filter the gallery.
- **The Layout:** A large horizontal highlight banner appears at the *top* of the gallery page before the grid of hairs begins.
- **Visuals:** Instead of just one image, the banner showcases 3 different angles (Front, Side, Back) of one *highly popular/representative* hairstyle from that category.
- **Purpose:** It instantly confirms to the user what category they are in and sets a premium, lookbook-style tone before they start scrolling through individual stylists.

## 🖼️ Section: Hairstyle Detail Page

**Concept Name:** "Mixed Media Gallery (Video + Image) - Inspired by Fabletics"

**1. Reference URL:** `https://www.fabletics.com/products/THE-ONE-SHORT-LINED-7IN-SO2500518-13411`
**2. The Vibe:**
- **What to Keep:** We are ONLY taking the idea of mixing high-quality images and short, silent video loops in the same gallery. The video serves to show the hair from different angles dynamically.
- **What to Reject:** We are NOT using their vertical scrolling layout. The UI will be cleaner and more standard (e.g., a main focus area with smaller selectable angles).
- **Stylist Discovery (How it Connects):** Once the user is looking at this detailed gallery (with the video and images), the *same page* will feature a section below it querying the Google Maps API: "Stylists In Your Area Who Do This Style."

## 🎭 Section: Copywriting & Narrative (The Core Story)

**Concept Name:** "It's All About You (The Transformation)"

**1. The True Narrative:**
- GlamGo isn't just about hair; it's about the **emotional journey of transformation**. It's about a woman deciding who she wants to be for the upcoming week, a special event, or a new phase in life. 
- The website must feel intensely empathetic and client-centric. We are here to help her safely and confidently choose her new look.

**2. Delivery: Scroll-Scrub Text Reveal (The Nikola Radeski Effect)**
- **Reference URL:** `https://nikolaradeski.com/`
- **The Execution:** Instead of throwing a giant paragraph of text at the user about our "chemistry and story," we make them *read it at the speed they scroll*. The paragraph starts out faded. As the user scrolls down, the words turn bold/black one by one, like someone is highlighting or reading along with them.
- **Why it fits:** It is incredibly satisfying to scroll through. It forces the user to actually read the core message because the animation pulls their eyes along the sentence.
