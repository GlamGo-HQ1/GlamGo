## THE PROBLEM

## What's Happening Right Now

There are over 50 million Nigerian women who make their hair regularly — some weekly, most at least monthly. Finding and booking a good hair stylist is still done through inefficient methods:

1. **WhatsApp Status** — Stylists post pictures (often stolen from bigger stylists' Instagram pages). Quality is inconsistent. You can't tell if the stylist actually did that hair or just downloaded the picture.
2. **Instagram** — Bigger stylists build followings and post their work. But discovery depends on followers, hashtags, and the algorithm. Small but talented stylists are invisible.
3. **Word of mouth** — "My friend knows someone who does good hair." This works but it's slow and limited to your social circle.
4. **Walking into a salon** — You go to a salon, see what they can do, hope for the best. No way to preview quality before committing.

## The Core Frustrations

### For Clients (Women)

- You see a hairstyle picture on WhatsApp/Instagram but you can't tell if the stylist actually did it or stole the picture
- You have no way to compare stylists objectively
- Booking is done through DMs and calls — no structured scheduling
- You don't know the price until you're already in the chair
- If the hair comes out wrong, there's no accountability
- Finding a stylist who does home service is luck-based

### For Hair Stylists

- Getting new clients depends on your Instagram following or your shop's foot traffic
- Small talented stylists are invisible because they can't afford marketing or a good shop location
- If you work in someone's shop, they take the biggest cut of your earnings
- You can't build a personal brand or reputation outside of word of mouth
- No structured way to manage bookings — it's all WhatsApp back-and-forth

## THE SOLUTION

## What We're Building

A platform that does for hair styling what Chowdeck did for food delivery.

**But our edge is NOT just booking.** Several apps already do basic booking (onfleekQ, Beauty Butler, Paddim). Our edge is the **VISUAL EXPERIENCE.**

When a woman opens our app, she doesn't see a list of stylists. She sees a **stunning gallery of hairstyles** — presented like a fashion magazine, not a WhatsApp status dump. Every style is showcased with premium, consistent, high-quality presentation.

She browses. She finds a style she loves. She taps it. She sees verified stylists near her who can do THAT EXACT STYLE — with their real portfolio, ratings, and prices. She books. She pays. The stylist comes to her house. Done.

> **The pitch line: "We're building the Chowdeck experience for beauty — where the visual presentation IS the product."**

## DATA MODEL (Simplified)

## Users

- id, email, name, phone, role (client/stylist), avatar_url
- location (city, area), created_at

## Stylist Profiles

- id, user_id, bio, rating (average), total_reviews
- service_area, is_verified, is_available
- specialties (array: braids, locs, weaves, etc.)

## Hairstyles

- id, name, category, description
- images (array of URLs), price_range_min, price_range_max
- estimated_duration_minutes

## Stylist Styles (what each stylist can do)

- stylist_id, style_id, price, portfolio_images

## Bookings

- id, client_id, stylist_id, style_id
- date, time_slot, status (pending/confirmed/completed/cancelled)
- client_location, total_price
- payment_status, payment_reference

## Reviews

- id, booking_id, client_id, stylist_id
- rating (1-5), comment, created_at
