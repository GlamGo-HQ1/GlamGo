## Section 2: STORY (Scroll-Scrub Text Reveal)

This is the paragraph that fades in word-by-word as the user scrolls. It needs to be short, emotional, and punchy.

### ⭐ Option A (Transformation — my pick):

> *You already know who you want to be. You can see her. The hair, the confidence, the walk. GlamGo brings her to life — from the stylist who truly understands your vision.*

**Why:** It speaks directly to the emotional moment before a woman books an appointment. It's personal, confident, and ends on the value prop (the right stylist).

### Option B (Discovery):

> *Finding the right stylist shouldn't feel like a gamble. See every angle before you commit. Book with confidence. Walk out exactly how you imagined.*

**Why:** More practical. Leads with the pain point (uncertainty), then solves it. Less emotional, more logical.

### Option C (Empowerment):

> *Your hair tells your story. Every braid, every curl, every cut — a chapter. GlamGo connects you with stylists who don't just do hair. They understand vision.*

**Why:** Poetic. Good for brand building. Slightly longer — might feel heavy in the scroll reveal.
